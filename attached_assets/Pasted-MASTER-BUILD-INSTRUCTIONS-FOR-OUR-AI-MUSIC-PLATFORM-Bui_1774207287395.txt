MASTER BUILD INSTRUCTIONS FOR OUR AI MUSIC PLATFORM

Build a production-grade, commercial-quality AI music intelligence, arrangement, orchestration, rendering, and export platform.

This is not a prototype and not a demo.
This must be built as a real flagship product with clean architecture, reliable backend services, advanced audio intelligence, premium UI, and a modular pipeline that can grow into a world-class music platform.

The system must be capable of:

ingesting full songs or vocal-only recordings
analyzing all core musical elements
inferring harmony at a high musical level
arranging and orchestrating in multiple genres
supporting arranger-style profiles
generating intros, endings, transitions, and modulations
rendering audio
exporting notation and production assets
providing a premium DAW-like user experience

Do not skip steps.
Do not collapse everything into one service.
Do not hide fake simulation behind real processing.
Build the system in the exact order below.

never treat preview rendering as final professional rendering
